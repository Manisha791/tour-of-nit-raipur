courses we offer

The National Institute of Technology, Raipur offers admissions for several UG, PG, and Ph.D. programs.

Undergraduate

NIT Raipur admission to 41 courses at the UG, PG, and doctorate levels is conducted through national-level entrance examinations. At the UG Level, the institute offers BTech and BArch programs for the duration of 4 and 5 years respectively. Admission to both UG programs is based on JEE Main scores and JoSAA counseling. The eligible students should register for the JoSAA counseling for their seat allocation after the result declaration of JEE Main exam. Usually, JOSAA counseling for B.Tech admissions takes place in the month of July/ August. The total course fee for the NIT Raipur B.Tech program is INR 5.52 Lakhs. For NIT Raipur B.Tech in CSE stream, the cut off ranges around 9490 for the general category.

Post-graduate

NIT Raipur also offers admission to PG courses such M.Tech, and M.CA in Applied Geology. Admission to the two-year M.Tech and MCA programs at NIT Raipur is offered via GATE and NIMCET entrance scores followed by counseling. Candidates from outside India are selected for these courses through Direct Admission of Students from Abroad (DASA)

Ph.D

Along with the UG and PG programs, NIT Raipur provides Ph.D. programs under various disciplines. Shortlisting for Ph.D. admission is done on the basis of an institute based entrance exam institute followed by a PI round. However, candidates having valid scores in GATE/ CEED/ UGC/ CSIR are also considered eligible for NIT Raipur Ph.D. Programme

our facilities

The university has excellent IT Infrastructure facility for the students. The Coffee Day beverage centre functioning at the NIT campus is open on all weekdays, from 8 am to 10 pm. The sports department provides facilities for outdoor games like Cricket, Football, Hockey, Basketball, Badminton, Baseball, Lawn Tennis, Gymnastics, Volleyball, etc. The NIT Raipur dispensary is situated near the side of the NCC office. Presently there are two girls' hostels on the campus and a proposal for one more of 200 seaters is also in process. The institute has 5 boys' hostel within the campus. Hostels A, B, C, D and F have a dining hall cum recreation hall equipped with TV, table tennis and carrom, etc.
